		<h1>Welcome to<br /><span>SKY ASYLUM</span></h1>
		<p>We are a New England-based indie film company. Since our start in 2002, we have worked in feature and documentary formats, as well as special events and weddings.</p>
		
		<p>Sky Asylum exists to shine the light of the truth of creation and our Creator through the medium of film. It exists to hold a mirror up to the beauty, joy and pain of the every day; reflecting the black and white and full color of the wide spectrum of life.</p>
	
		<p>In addition to our own productions, we have worked in association with Purple Crayon Productions for the sci-fi The Present (accepted at festivals in New York and New Hampshire), and with Fox C. Flix for the crime thriller, Dominoes.</p>
		
		<p>Made of a team of poets, dreamers, pirates and adventurers, Sky Asylum will continue to make quality films as long as those movies are relevant and armed with purpose.</p>
